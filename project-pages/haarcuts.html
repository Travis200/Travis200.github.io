Description
One of my projects outside of university was building a cross-platform mobile barbering application. A small student start-up approached me to develop the project. For this project, I led a small team consisting of three developers including myself. This application was developed using the Flutter framework and Dart and allows clients to see the location of local barbers through the integration of a google maps plugin. Unfortunately midway through the project, the client discontinued the project due to financial reasons. However, I have still gained valuable experience and insight into developing software for a client from this experience.
Technologies Used
  • Dart
  • Flutter
  • Firebase
